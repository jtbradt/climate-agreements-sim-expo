# CONFIDENTIAL BRIEFING: Sub-Saharan Africa

**Your delegation represents Sub-Saharan Africa (Nigeria, Ethiopia, Kenya, Tanzania, South Africa and others).**

> All dollar figures are in 2024 US dollars (World Bank WDI). Emissions data from EDGAR/IEA (2024). Temperature and vulnerability data from Burke, Hsiang & Miguel (2015).

---

## Economic Profile
- GDP per capita: ~$1,500 (the lowest among all delegations)
- Economic growth rate: ~2.5% per year
- Population: 1.29 billion (and growing rapidly -- the fastest population growth of any delegation)

## Emissions Profile
- Current emissions: 2.4 Gt CO2e/year -- **the smallest emitter by far**
- Trend: Emissions are growing (~2.5%/year) from a very low base as economies develop
- Your per-capita emissions are negligible compared to developed countries

## Climate Vulnerability
- Your average temperature (22.7 C) is **well above the economic optimum**
- You face **the most severe climate damages** of any delegation -- droughts, crop failures, heat stress, and displacement
- Climate change threatens to undo decades of development progress
- Your countries have contributed almost nothing to the problem historically

## Abatement Costs
- You have good renewable energy resources and low abatement costs in principle
- But you have **very limited financial capacity** and competing development priorities (healthcare, education, infrastructure)
- You should not be expected to bear significant abatement costs

## Negotiating Priorities
- **You have the moral high ground**: You emit the least and suffer the most
- Demand compensation for climate damages (loss and damage) from the countries that caused the problem
- Demand financial transfers to fund adaptation AND mitigation
- Build alliances with India -- you share similar equity arguments
- Frame the negotiation as a justice issue, not just an economic one

## Suggested Red Line
Do not accept any deal that does not include substantial transfer payments TO Sub-Saharan Africa (at least $10B/year). You should NOT be a net payer under any scenario.
