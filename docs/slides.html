How It Works

Round 1: Opening Plenary

Review your briefing sheet. Prepare an opening offer. Each chief negotiator presents to the room.

5 min prep + 5 min speeches

Round 2: Bilateral Talks

Structured bilateral meetings. Negotiate side deals. Revise your offer based on what you learn.

12--15 min

Round 3: Final Plenary

Last chance to close a deal. Final offers are entered. Can this body deliver?

8--10 min

Ground Rules

  • Only chief negotiators speak in plenary. Advisors pass notes.
  • Your briefing sheet is confidential. Share strategically — you don't have to be truthful.
  • Transfers must balance: total in = total out. (The tool will warn you if not.)
  • There is no enforcement. Agreements are voluntary — just like real COPs.
  • When the timer hits zero, submit your offer sheet — whatever is on it is your position.
